About Us

Travel budget prediction involves estimating the total cost of a trip based on various factors. This helps travelers plan their finances and enables travel agencies to offer personalized services. Here are some key aspects to consider:

Factors Affecting Travel Budget

Destination: The cost of travel varies significantly depending on the destination. Popular tourist destinations tend to be more expensive.

Accommodation: The type of accommodation you choose (hotel, hostel, Airbnb) and the desired level of comfort will impact your budget.

Transportation: The mode of transportation (flight, train, bus, car rental) and the distance traveled will influence the cost.

Food: Your dietary preferences, eating habits, and the local cost of food will affect your food budget.
